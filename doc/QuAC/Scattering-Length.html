5.2 Scattering Length

The scattering length is calculated by the asymptotic analysis of the zero energy and zero angular momentum wave function at large distances. It behaves as u_0(r) = Ar + B, with a = - B/A.

The scattering length can also be computed by the integration of the log-derivative equation, as described by Meshkov (REF). This is done using ‘MESHKOV’ as method of scattering length (see Scattering Length Method).
