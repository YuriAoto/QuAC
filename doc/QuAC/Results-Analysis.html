5.4 Results Analysis

The calculations carried by Phase Shifts or Bound States calculations can be analysed in this job. The analysis implemented are the calculation of the total, partial and differential cross sections, for Phase Shifts results, and vibrational transition intensities, Dunhan parameters and vibrationally averaged equilibrium distances for Bound States results. Only one analysis is carried by each Results Analysis job and must be asked by the keyword AnalysisType (see Analysis Type).
