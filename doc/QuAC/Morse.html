4.5 Morse

The parameters of a Morse potential can be given explicitly, by the type Morse, or as the ones which gives the same equilibrium distance, dissociation energy and force constant of the natural cubic splines over a given set of points, by the name AppMorse. Expression:

V(r) = D[(1 - exp(-B(r - r_0))) - 1]

General syntax: 

radius epsilon beta

or 

r_1 V_1
r_2 V_2
...
r_n V_n

radius = r_0, epsilon = D, beta = B. The second case is for ‘AppMorse’ type.
