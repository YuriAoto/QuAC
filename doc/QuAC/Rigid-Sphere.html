4.1 Rigid Sphere

The rigid sphere potential, HardSphere, is zero valued after a given distance, the radius, and the wave function must completely vanish at distances below the radius. Only for this type of potential the unit of energy is not required.

Expression:

u_l(r) = 0.0 if r <= r_0
V(r) = 0.0 if r > r_0

General syntax: 

radius

radius = r_0
