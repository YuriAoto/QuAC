5.3 Bound States

The vibrational bound states can be obtained by the second order algorithm of Cooley and Lowdin (REF). They can be calculated in three ways. QuAC can calculate exactly the ones required, by the keywords VibLevel or VibLevelMax (see Vibrational Levels), use the energies given as starting points for the algorithm or calculate all the vibrational levels, if neither the energies nor the vibrational levels were given. QuAC automatically stores the energy, vibrational level, number of iterations and wave function for posterior analysis.
