7.3.17 Meshkov Parameters

The Meshkov parameters, r_bar and beta (See Scattering Length.), are required for a Meshkov procedure for scattering length calculation. It can be given with the keyword MeshkovParam.

General syntax: 

MeshkovParam r_bar beta

The r_bar and beta arguments are the Meshkov parameters and r_bar must be given in the units used in the potential of the current job.

Incompatibilities:

Have meaning only for scattering length calculations by the Meshkov procedure.

Default values:

Do not have default values. Must be given in a Meshkov calculation.
