4.6 Murrel Sorbie

A Murrel Sorbie type of potential is defined by the keyword MurSorbie. Expression:

V(r) = D[1 + sum[_(i=1)^n](a_i(r-r_0)^i)]exp[-a_1(r-r_0)]

General syntax: 

radius epsilon
a_1
a_2
...
a_n

radius = r_0, epsilon = D, a_i = a_i, i=1,2,...,n.
