7.4.5 Frank Condon

The vibrational intensity with the Frank Condon approximation can be carried using the keyword FrankCondon.

General syntax: 

FrankCondon ave_dip ave_dip_unit

Here the ave_dip and ave_dip_unit arguments are the averaged electric transition dipole moment and its unit, respectively.

Incompatibilities:

Have meaning only for ‘VibrationalIntensity’ analysis performed with two vibrational jobs. It is incompatible with the keyword ElecDipoleFunction. Default values:

By default the Frank Condon approximation is not assumed.
