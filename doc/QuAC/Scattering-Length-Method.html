7.3.16 Scattering Length Method

One of the two methods available to calculate the scattering length can be specified by the keyword ScattLenMeth.(See Scattering Length.)

General syntax: 

ScattLenMeth method

method can be ‘DIRECT’ or ‘MESHKOV’.

Incompatibilities:

Have meaning only for SCATT_LENGTH calculation.

Default values:

The default method is the ‘DIRECT’.
